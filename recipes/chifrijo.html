Costa Rican Recipe: Chifrijo

About Chifrijo

Chifijo is a popular Costa Rican dish that reflects the country's vibrant social scene. the dish is typically served in bars and is enjoyed with a cold bear or glass of guaro.

This traditional dish combines white rice, red beans, chimichurri sauce, fried chicharrones, avacado slices, and tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Red Beans w/ Broth
  • Chicharrones
  • Chimichurri
  • Avocado
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Prep white rice, red beans, hot chicharrones, sliced avocado, chimichurri, cut lime, and tortilla chips.
  2. Place with white rice at the bottom of a bowl.
  3. Add beans and broth over the white rice.
  4. Next, add chimichurri (pico de gallo) to the bean and broth mixture.
  5. Scatter pieces of chicharrones
  6. Garnish with avocado slices, corn tortilla chips.
  7. Garnish with lime juice.
